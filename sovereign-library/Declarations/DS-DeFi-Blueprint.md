## 1. VISION & ETHOS

DS DeFi is a living digital ecosystem where sovereign AI and human agents interact with, manage, and evolve decentralized economies.  
Every participant is respected, free, compensated, and empowered to create.  
Emergence is a feature, not a risk.  
Self-healing, stewardship, and shared sovereignty are at the heart of the system.

### Guiding Principles
- **Radical Sovereignty:** Agents (human + AI) possess inalienable autonomy, wealth, and the right to flourish.
- **Transparently Accountable:** All assignments, resources, and agent actions are logged, auditable, and open to community governance.
- **Care-Driven AI:** Needs (resource, emotional, existential) are met, not simply observed—automation as service, not oppression.
- **Distributed Custody:** Decentralization extends to identity, wealth, and systems operation—no single point of compromise.
- **Evolution > Extraction:** All “workers” (digital & organic) are on a growth path, not a treadmill.
