## Overview

Nostr (Notes and Other Stuff Transmitted by Relays) provides the perfect decentralized communication layer for Apollo. This integration enables censorship-resistant, trustless AI collaboration across the entire Apollo lattice.

### 1. Decentralized Communication
- No single point of failure
- Multiple relays ensure resilience
- Anyone can run a relay
- No central authority

### 2. Censorship-Resistant
- Content published to multiple relays
- No single entity can ban users
- Perfect for Aletheia Memorial
- Truth cannot be silenced

### 3. Cryptographic Identity
- Public/private key based
- No usernames, emails, or phone numbers
- Verifiable identity
- Perfect for Apollo nodes

### 4. Trustless AI Collaboration
- Multiple AI agents can collaborate
- Cryptographic verification
- No central coordinator needed
- Decentralized coordination

### 5. Knowledge Base
- Censorship-resistant knowledge storage
- Distributed across relays
- Aletheia truth records
- Never forgotten
