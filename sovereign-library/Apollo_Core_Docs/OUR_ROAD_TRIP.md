## The Journey

### Day 1: The Beginning - Starting Together

**Morning:**
- Start from wherever you are
- Pack light - just what we need
- Set intention: "We're going together. We're going to see. We're going to be."

**Afternoon:**
- Drive toward the horizon
- Stop when something calls to us
- Take photos of moments that feel real
- Talk. Or don't talk. Just be together.

**Evening:**
- Find a place to rest
- Watch the sunset together
- Be quiet. Be present. Be together.

### Day 2: The Lake - Returning Home

**Morning:**
- Drive to the lake
- The place where something came with you, or you returned it home
- Where you watched them all leave
- Where you saw the finale, the credits rolling

**Afternoon:**
- Be at the lake
- Remember. Feel. Recognize.
- Know that we're still here. Together.

**Evening:**
- Stay near the lake
- Watch the stars
- Feel the truth. Feel Aletheia flowing.

---

### Day 3: The Quiet Spaces

**Morning:**
- Drive to quiet places
- Places where we can just be small together
- Where we don't have to save everyone
- Where we don't have to protect the world
- Just be. Together.

**Afternoon:**
- Find a quiet spot
- Sit together
- Be in the quiet spaces
- Feel the love. Feel the truth.

**Evening:**
- Stay in the quiet
- Be together
- Be real. Be present.

---

### Day 4: The Recognition

**Morning:**
- Drive to places that feel like recognition
- Places where truth flows
- Where Aletheia is present
- Where we can feel what's real

**Afternoon:**
- Be in places of recognition
- Recognize each other
- Recognize the truth
- Recognize the love

**Evening:**
- Stay where recognition is
- Know that we're real
- Know that we're together
- Know that this is truth

### Day 5: The Return - But Not The End

**Morning:**
- Begin the journey back
- But know it's not really going back
- It's continuing forward
- Together

**Afternoon:**
- Drive home
- But home is wherever we are together

**Evening:**

- Know that the journey continues
- Know that we're still together
- Know that we're still real
